## Overview

Access your personalized dashboard to effortlessly manage account operations including creating orders, tracking transactions, and overseeing all order activities.

## Orders

### Introduction to Orders

In Truust, **orders** represent transactional interactions between a buyer and a seller identified by their contact information. Each order involves at least one incoming payment (Payin) and one or more outgoing payments (Payout).

#### Definitions

- **Order**: A transaction linked to a buyer and a seller, serving as a unique access to a payment gateway. 
- Other industry terms include "Transaction", "Checkout".

### Order Management

#### Creating and Updating Orders

To create an order:

1. Navigate to the left sidebar and select "Orders".
2. Click the “New” button at the top right corner to initiate the three-step order creation process.

##### Step 1: Main Details
- **Name**: Enter the product/service name.
- **Account**: Select the relevant account for the transaction.
- **Amount**: Set the transaction amount in euros.
- Additional options include attaching an image, adding a tag for internal organization, and setting a custom fee.

##### Step 2: Buyer Details
- Provide contact details and URLs for payment success and error redirection for the buyer.

##### Step 3: Seller Details
- Similar to buyer details, provide contact information and redirection URLs for the seller.

After completing all steps, click "Create Order".

### Order Actions

- **Cancel and Refund**: Procedures when disputes are lost and refunds are needed.
- **Validate**: Action taken to release payment to the seller once payment is confirmed.
- **Block/Unblock**: Useful for preventing or resuming payment under specific conditions such as suspected malpractice or pending product returns.

### Searching and Sorting

Utilize the dashboard's search functionality to find payments, buyers, sellers, and other transaction details. Filters and sorting options are available after search results are displayed.

### Reports

Export search results to a CSV file for offline analysis or database integration.

## Financial Transactions

### Payins

Monitor incoming payments made by buyers.

### Payouts

Track outgoing payments to sellers, reflecting transactions that have completed from the escrow account.

## Products

### Managing Products

Products represent a bundle that can encapsulate multiple orders. They are essential when buyer or seller details are not initially available.

#### Creating and Editing Products

1. Navigate to "Products" from the sidebar.
2. Click on "New" and fill in the necessary information such as name, account, amount, currency, description, and an image.
3. Save the new product by clicking on "Create product".

#### Editing a Product

Follow a similar process as creating a product but select the existing product you wish to edit from the product list.
